Short Biography

Jongik Kim received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science from KAIST, and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Seoul National University. He worked as a senior researcher with ETRI, and he was a professor at Jeonbuk National University. He is currently a professor with Chungnam National University. His research interests are in the field of data management, including query processing and optimization, text and graph data analytics, and storage systems.
